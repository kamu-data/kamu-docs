This is a self-guided tour of `kamu` that will teach you the basics of Kamu CLI and Kamu Node and will teach you the core data science concepts behind it, without needing to install anything.

Be ready to dedicate ~30 minutes to it.

Get started at: https://demo.kamu.dev

<Warning>
The capacity of this environment is limited and you may experience worse performance than running `kamu` on your machine.
</Warning>
